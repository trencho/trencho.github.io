About Me

I'm a software engineer focused on backend and data engineering. I've designed and shipped RESTful APIs and, more recently, large-scale ETL pipelines — building data workflows on Azure Databricks and Apache Spark for the insurance sector, after years of Java/Spring development across banking (3DS secure payments), telecommunications and healthcare.

My work spans the full delivery cycle: modelling data and APIs, containerising with Docker and Kubernetes, and shipping through CI/CD. I've collaborated directly with international clients across Europe and the US, translating business requirements into maintainable, production-ready systems.

I hold a Master's in Electrical Engineering and Information Technologies, where my thesis on monitoring atmospheric impacts and predicting air pollution grew into open-source machine-learning projects. I value clean, well-tested code, pragmatic design and continuous learning — and I'm comfortable explaining technical trade-offs to both engineers and non-technical stakeholders.
